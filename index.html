About Us

Nurtured within the academic landscape of Oxford College Butwal, Pathya Pustak Sangathan sprouted from a palpable discontentment among students regarding the quality of education and the responsiveness of the college administration. Frustrated by what they perceived as subpar teaching standards and a lack of receptivity to student concerns, the founders of the organization sought to chart their educational journey independently. Motivated by a collective desire to transcend the limitations of the traditional classroom environment, they forged a path towards self-directed learning and collaboration.

However, it's important to note that this dissatisfaction was not solely aimed at Oxford College itself, but rather at the broader issues perceived within the educational system. As their initiative gained traction, fueled by a shared passion for technology and consultancy, Pathya Pustak Sangathan blossomed into a beacon of innovation and collective empowerment. Today, it stands as a testament to the transformative potential of proactive engagement and community-driven education, offering a compelling alternative to traditional educational models.
